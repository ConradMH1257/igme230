Goal/Audience

The goal of this site is to have a location for people to view my work and see what kind of skills I have.
The main audience would be Lawley and my family. The next type of audience would be recruiters for companies.

Design Decision

I wanted something that was not normal for website and bring out what type of designer I am.
Some of the decisions you see are the colors for the site itself. Menu layout was changed from being horizontal bar to a vertical bar that would open and close when clicked

Sources

Most of the items came from my past projects with the need to look up what I needed to know. I did need to use W3schools for some simple stuff on the first attempt of this website.

Changes Made

What I changed on this website first was the color of the site it self. It was blue, now it is more a light brown.
Another change that was made was the menu. I added a drop down menu and changed the layout from being horizontal to vertical. I wanted to add a 3d viewer for my models and a youtube video of my classes capstone. I did fix a number of errors I had before though the art page does have some problems with validation.
